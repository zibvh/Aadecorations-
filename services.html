Interior Painting

Transform your living spaces with flawless finishes that last for years.

Our interior painting service goes beyond just applying colour. We begin with meticulous preparation – sanding, filling, and priming – to ensure a perfect base. We protect your floors, furniture, and fittings with dust sheets and masking tape, leaving no mess behind.

Whether you're refreshing a single room or renovating your entire home, our team delivers crisp lines, smooth finishes, and attention to every detail. We use premium paints that resist fading and wear, ensuring your walls look beautiful for years to come.

What's Included:

  • Wall & ceiling painting
  • Woodwork (skirting, doors, frames)
  • Radiator & pipe painting
  • Filling & surface preparation
  • Premium paint brands (Farrow & Ball, Dulux Trade, etc.)
  • Full protection & clean-up

Exterior Painting

Protect and enhance your property's kerb appeal with weather-resistant finishes.

Your home's exterior takes a beating from the Welsh weather. Our exterior painting service uses specialist, breathable masonry paints that protect against rain, wind, and UV while allowing moisture to escape. We properly prepare all surfaces – pressure washing, scraping loose paint, and priming – for a finish that lasts.

From rendered walls to wooden fascias and windows, we work safely and efficiently. We're fully insured and use professional scaffolding or ladders where needed.

What's Included:

  • Masonry & render painting
  • Woodwork (windows, doors, fascias)
  • Pressure washing & preparation
  • Weather-resistant paints
  • Gutter & downpipe painting
  • 5-7 year durability guarantee

Wallpaper Services

Professional hanging and removal – from feature walls to full rooms.

Wallpaper requires skill and patience. Our decorators are experts in pattern matching, mitring corners, and achieving seamless joins. We can hang everything from modern feature wallpapers to traditional patterns.

For wallpaper removal, we use industrial steamers that lift old paper without damaging the plaster beneath. We'll then prepare the walls perfectly for painting or re-papering.

Services Include:

  • Wallpaper hanging (all types)
  • Pattern matching & lining paper
  • Feature wall installation
  • Steam stripping & removal
  • Wall preparation & sizing
