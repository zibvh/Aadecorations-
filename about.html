Our Story

Craftsmanship Rooted in South Wales

AA Decorating was founded in 2012 by Andrew Adams, a local decorator with a passion for perfection.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted team of skilled decorators serving Barry, Penarth, Cardiff, and Newport. But our values haven't changed: we still treat every home like our own, and we never compromise on quality.

Andrew trained under master decorators in London before returning to Wales to start his own business. He built AA Decorating on the principle that proper preparation and premium materials are the only way to achieve a finish that lasts.

Today, we're proud to be one of South Wales's most recommended decorating companies. Our team shares Andrew's commitment to punctuality, cleanliness, and craftsmanship. We're fully insured, continuously trained, and genuinely care about the work we do.
